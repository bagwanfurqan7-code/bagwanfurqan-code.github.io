Siddheshwar Temple

Siddheshwar Temple is one of the most important spiritual landmarks in Solapur. Dedicated to Lord Siddheshwar, a form of Lord Shiva.

The temple is located near Siddheshwar Lake in the center of the city. Thousands of devotees visit the temple every day.

The temple becomes especially crowded during the Siddheshwar Yatra festival held every year in January.

Pandharpur

Pandharpur is one of the most sacred pilgrimage towns in Maharashtra located about 70 km from Solapur.

It is famous for the Vitthal Rukmini Temple dedicated to Lord Vitthal. Millions of devotees participate in the Wari pilgrimage during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi festivals.

Akkalkot

Akkalkot is another spiritual destination located about 40 km from Solapur. It is famous for the temple of Shri Swami Samarth Maharaj.

Devotees believe Swami Samarth blesses people and helps them overcome difficulties in life.

Solapur Fort

Solapur Fort also called Bhuikot Fort is a historic monument built during the Bahmani Sultanate period.

The structure represents medieval military architecture and the rich historical heritage of the region.
